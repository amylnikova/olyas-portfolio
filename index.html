Hi, my name is Olya and I'm a designer from Kyiv. I'm mainly engaged in graphic design, but recently I've been trying my hand at UI/UX.

I like creating interesting visuals, experimenting with shapes and colors, finding new meanings by delving deeper into the search. I believe that a good designer is a researcher. A researcher is always immersed into a subject, collecting unique ideas, analyzing a lot of authentic information to invent new visual tools and approaches that will make everyone look at familiar things from a different angle.

My ideal design is when a project creates a new visual language. So far, I'm only striving for this level.
